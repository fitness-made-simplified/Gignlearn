What are Body Systems? 🤔

Our body has many amazing systems — each one does a special job to keep us healthy and alive!

🧠

Nervous System

The command center! Your brain sends messages to every part of your body.

BrainNervesSpinal Cord
❤️

Circulatory System

Your heart pumps blood through tubes called arteries and veins — like a super pump!

HeartBloodArteries
🫁

Respiratory System

Every breath you take brings oxygen in and pushes carbon dioxide out!

LungsTracheaNose
🍔

Digestive System

Breaks down your food into tiny bits the body can use for energy!

StomachLiverIntestines
🦴

Skeletal System

Your skeleton — 206 bones hold your body up and protect your organs!

BonesJointsCartilage
💪

Muscular System

Over 600 muscles let you run, jump, smile — and keep your heart beating!

MusclesTendons
💛

Excretory System

Gets rid of waste so your body stays clean and healthy inside!

KidneysUrinarySkin
🌿

Endocrine System

Tiny chemical messengers (hormones) that control growth and mood!

ThyroidPancreas

💡 Fun Facts

Did You Know? 🤩

🧠

Your brain has about 100 BILLION neurons — that's more than all the stars you can see in the night sky!

🔬 Neuroscience fact

❤️

Your heart beats about 100,000 times every single day — that's 35 million times a year!

🫀 Cardiology fact

🫁

If you unfolded all the tiny air sacs in your lungs, they would cover a whole tennis court!

🌬️ Pulmonology fact

🦴

Babies are born with about 300 bones, but adults only have 206 — some bones join together as we grow!

🦴 Anatomy fact

🫘

Your small intestine is actually 6 metres long — about as tall as four grown-ups standing on top of each other!

🍔 Digestive fact

💛

Your two kidneys filter all the blood in your body about 40 times every day — like the world's best cleaning machines!

💧 Nephrology fact
