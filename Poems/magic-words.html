Poem Lyrics 🎵

Read every line out loud with your child — or press the button and let Gigloo read it for you!

1
Hello, hello, we wave and say,
"Good morning friends, let's start the day!"
2
When we want, we say "Please, please!"
Like magic keys, they open with ease.
3
If we hurt, we say "Sorry, friend,"
It makes the fight come to an end.
4
"Thank you, thank you," bright and sweet,
Magic words make hearts beat–beat! ❤️

What Your Child Learns

Every GigNLearn poem is designed with a clear educational goal. Here's what this poem builds.

📚

Vocabulary Learned

  • Hello — greeting others warmly
  • Please — asking politely
  • Sorry — taking responsibility
  • Thank You — expressing gratitude
  • Good Morning — daily greetings
🧠

Skills Developed

  • Social-emotional language
  • Rhyme & rhythm awareness
  • Phonemic reading readiness
  • Memory through repetition
  • Empathy & emotional awareness
🎯

Curriculum Fit

  • Preschool & Kindergarten level
  • Social Studies — manners
  • English Language — vocabulary
  • SEL — Social Emotional Learning
⏱️

Usage Guide

  • Read time: ~3 minutes
  • Activity time: ~15 minutes
  • Best for: Ages 2–6 years
  • Repeat daily for best results

💡 Try This Activity at Home!

After reading, play a role-play game with your child. Act out different situations — someone offers a cookie, someone bumps into you, someone gives a gift — and ask your child: "Which magic word do we say now?" This turns the poem into real-life practice! 🎭
