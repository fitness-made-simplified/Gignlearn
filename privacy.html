🔒 Privacy Policy

Gig N Learn · gignlearn.fun

📅 Last updated: April 2026  |  Effective date: January 2026

Welcome to Gig N Learn ("we," "us," or "our"), accessible at gignlearn.fun. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website. We are committed to protecting your privacy, especially regarding children's safety.

🐯 Gig N Learn is a free educational website for children aged 2–8. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our content is child-safe and ad-free (at this time).

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to operate our website effectively:

  • Usage Data: When you visit our site, we may automatically collect data such as your browser type, device type, pages visited, and time spent on pages. This is collected through analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to help us improve our content.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us via email (gignlearn@gmail.com) or WhatsApp, we collect the information you provide (name, email, message) solely to respond to your enquiry.
  • No Account Registration: Our website does not require users to create accounts, and we do not collect passwords or login credentials.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To improve our website content and user experience
  • To understand how visitors use our site (analytics)
  • To respond to your messages and enquiries
  • To monitor website performance and fix technical issues

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Cookies

Our website may use cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your browsing experience. These include:

  • Analytics cookies (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand website traffic
  • Functional cookies to remember your preferences

You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of our site.

If we display Google AdSense advertisements in the future, Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to or embed content from the following third-party services:

  • YouTube — for embedded educational videos (governed by Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Google Drive — for PDF worksheet downloads
  • Google Analytics — for website analytics
  • Google AdSense — may be used in the future to display advertisements

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

5. Children's Privacy

Protecting children's privacy is extremely important to us. Our website is designed for children aged 2–8, used under parental supervision.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
  • We do not allow children to create accounts or submit personal data.
  • All content on our site is child-safe, educational, and appropriate for the stated age range.
  • If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at gignlearn@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure. Our website is hosted on secure platforms (GitHub Pages / Hostinger). However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at gignlearn@gmail.com.

8. External Links

Our website may contain links to external websites (YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
