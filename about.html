About GigNLearn

A free, joyful learning space for little ones — built by a parent, for parents and kids everywhere 💛

👨‍💻
Sushant
Founder & Creator · GigNLearn

Hi! I'm Sushant, the creator of GigNLearn and the human behind Gigloo the Tiger 🐯. As a parent and lifelong learner, I started GigNLearn because I wanted my child — and children everywhere — to have access to genuinely fun, high-quality, free learning resources.

I built every poem page, every interactive game, and every parenting guide on this site with care and intention. The content is informed by child development research, parenting literature, and the real-world experience of raising a young child. I work closely with educator communities and parent groups to ensure the material is both developmentally appropriate and genuinely engaging.

GigNLearn is my way of giving back — making the kind of resource I wish existed when I started my parenting journey.

👨‍👧 Parent 📚 Early Learning Enthusiast 💻 Web Developer 🧒 Kids Education Advocate 🇮🇳 Based in India

🎯 Our Mission

To make high-quality early childhood education freely accessible to every family — regardless of location, language, or financial background. Every child deserves to learn, play, and grow with joy.

Meet Gigloo the Tiger!

Gigloo is GigNLearn's friendly mascot — a curious, cheerful tiger cub who loves learning, singing, and making friends. Gigloo represents the spirit of joyful education: brave enough to try new things, kind enough to help others, and always ready for an adventure!

Every poem, game, and guide on this site comes with a little bit of Gigloo's magic 🌟

💛 Our Values

🆓

Free & Accessible

All core content on GigNLearn is and always will be freely accessible. We believe cost should never be a barrier to quality early education. The site is supported through advertising and optional affiliate links, which allow us to keep everything free.

🔒

Child-Safe First

Every page, game, and link on this site is curated to be 100% appropriate for young children. We do not feature violent, adult, or inappropriate content of any kind.

📚

Evidence-Informed

Our parenting guides and learning activities are informed by child development research, phonics education principles, and guidance from the early learning community.

🌍

For Every Family

GigNLearn is designed for families everywhere — India, the US, the UK, and beyond. We celebrate diverse cultures and create content that any family can enjoy together.

📢 Transparency & Disclosures

GigNLearn is a free educational website supported by advertising (including Google AdSense) and affiliate partnerships (including Amazon Associates). When you click on affiliate product links, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This helps us maintain the site and continue creating free content for children and families. All content recommendations are made independently — we only recommend products and resources we genuinely believe are safe and beneficial for kids.
