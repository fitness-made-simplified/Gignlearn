About Gig N Learn

Free, joyful, child-safe learning for every little one aged 2–8 🌈

🌟 Who We Are

Gig N Learn is a free educational website designed for children aged 2–8. We create original nursery rhymes, fun interactive games, printable worksheets, and parenting guides — all completely free and without any registration needed.

Our friendly mascot Gigloo the Tiger 🐯 guides children through every activity, making learning feel like an adventure rather than a chore.

🎯

Our Mission

Make quality early learning free and accessible to every family

👶

Our Audience

Children aged 2–8, parents, teachers & nursery educators

💚

Our Promise

Always free, always safe, always child-first

Original Content

📚 What Gig N Learn Offers

  • 🎵Nursery Rhymes & Original Poems — Sing-along videos, karaoke-style lyrics, and free PDF worksheets for every poem
  • 🎮Interactive Learning Games — ABC Tap & Play, Math Maze, and Name Label Maker — all browser-based and free
  • ✏️Free Printable Worksheets — Tracing books, vowels worksheets, colouring pages and more — no signup required
  • 👨‍👩‍👧Parents' Hub — Evidence-based articles on gentle parenting, early literacy, child development and sleep
  • ▶️YouTube Channel — Animated nursery rhyme videos updated regularly at @Gignlearnwithgigloo
