👨‍👩‍👧 Parents' Corner

Why this workbook is great for your child

🧠 Skills Developed

  • Fine motor control
  • Pen / pencil grip
  • Letter recognition
  • Number sense & counting
  • Hand-eye coordination

📚 Learning Outcomes

  • Recognise A–Z uppercase & lowercase
  • Write letters independently
  • Count and write numbers 1–10
  • Associate letters with words
  • Enjoy learning through colour

💡 Activity Tips

  • Start with just 2–3 pages per day
  • Praise effort, not just neatness
  • Sing the alphabet while tracing
  • Point out letters in everyday life
  • Make it fun — no pressure!
