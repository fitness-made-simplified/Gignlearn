Poem Lyrics ☀️

Read every bright line with your child — press the button and let Gigloo greet the morning!

☀️
Good morning, sun, up in the sky,
You shine so bright, oh way up high!
🐦
Birds are singing, trees all sway,
It's time to smile and start the day!
🪥
Brush my teeth and comb my hair,
Off to school with love and care!
🌸
Flowers bloom and dewdrops gleam,
Morning fresh like a lovely dream!
🍎
Eat my breakfast, pack my bag,
Wave goodbye — no need to drag!
🏫
Friends are waiting, let's all run,
A brand new day has just begun!
☀️
Good morning sun, good morning day,
We're happy, bright and ready to play! 🎉

What Your Child Learns

A poem that teaches AND builds healthy morning habits!

☀️

Vocabulary Learned

  • Nature: Sun, sky, birds, trees, flowers, dewdrops
  • Actions: Shine, sing, sway, smile, brush, comb
  • Feelings: Happy, bright, love, care, dream
  • Places: School, sky, home
🪥

Daily Habits

  • Morning routine — step by step
  • Dental hygiene — brush teeth!
  • Personal grooming — comb hair
  • Healthy breakfast before school
  • Positive attitude to school
🧠

Skills Developed

  • Phonics — sky/high, sway/day, hair/care
  • Sequencing — morning routine order
  • Nature awareness — sun, birds, trees
  • Emotional readiness for school
⏱️

Usage Guide

  • Read time: ~3 minutes
  • Best time: Every morning!
  • Best for: Ages 2–7 years
  • Print the chart — tick off each step!

💡 Make It a Morning Ritual!

Read or sing this poem every morning before school. After a week, your child will know it by heart! Make it a fun challenge — can they recite it while brushing their teeth? Print the Morning Routine Chart from the PDF and let your child tick off each step as they complete it. This builds independence and a positive start to every school day! 🌅✨
