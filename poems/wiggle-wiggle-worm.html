Why kids love this original wiggle poem!

  • Gross motor movement
  • Rhythm & body coordination
  • Left–right direction sense
  • Vocabulary: wiggle, polka, slide
  • Creative imaginative play

  • Wiggle · Worm · Dirt
  • Tiny · Glasses · Polka
  • Slide · Left · Right
  • Dancing · Wearing · Dotted
  • Every · Day · Night
