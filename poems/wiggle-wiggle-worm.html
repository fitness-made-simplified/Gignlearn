👨‍👩‍👧 Parents' Corner

Why kids love this original wiggle poem!

🧠 Skills Developed

  • Gross motor movement
  • Rhythm & body coordination
  • Left–right direction sense
  • Vocabulary: wiggle, polka, slide
  • Creative imaginative play

🌟 Key Words to Learn

  • Wiggle · Worm · Dirt
  • Tiny · Glasses · Polka
  • Slide · Left · Right
  • Dancing · Wearing · Dotted
  • Every · Day · Night

🕺 Activity Ideas

  • Do the wiggle dance together!
  • Draw and colour a wiggly worm
  • Make polka dot art with fingers
  • Find real worms in the garden
  • Make up more wiggle verses!
