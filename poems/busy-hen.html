🌾 The Poem

⭐ Original Poem · Gig N Learn

Cluck, cluck, cluck goes the hen,
Lays her eggs in the farmer's pen,
Wakes up early with the sun,
Her busy day has just begun!

👨‍👩‍👧 Parents' Corner

Why this little poem is big for learning!

🧠 Skills Developed

  • Phonics — rhyming words (hen/pen, sun/begun)
  • Nature & farm awareness
  • Sequencing — morning routine
  • Memory through repetition
  • Rhythm & beat recognition

🌟 Key Words to Learn

  • Cluck · Hen · Eggs
  • Farmer · Pen · Lays
  • Wakes · Early · Sunrise
  • Busy · Day · Begun

💡 Activity Ideas

  • Draw a hen and decorate her eggs
  • Make clucking sounds together!
  • Talk about morning routines
  • Visit a farm or watch farm videos
  • Make up a 2nd verse together!
