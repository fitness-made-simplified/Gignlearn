🎵 Poem Lyrics — Read & Sing Along!

🦁 Wild Animal — Lion

Roar roar, lion king so bold,
In the jungle brave and gold,
Lion sleeps under the sun,
Wild and free, everyone!

🐘 Wild Animal — Elephant

Trumpet loud, elephant grey,
Splashing water every day,
Big and strong with ears so wide,
Through the jungle, walks with pride!

🦒 Wild Animal — Giraffe

Neck so long, giraffe so tall,
Eating leaves above them all,
Spots of yellow, brown and more,
Tallest animal to adore!

🐯 Wild Animal — Tiger

Tiger tiger, stripes of black,
Creeping quietly, looking back,
In the forest, fast and bright,
Tiger prowls both day and night!

🦓 Wild Animal — Zebra

Zebra zebra, black and white,
Galloping from morning light,
Stripes upon the open plain,
Running free through sun and rain!

🌟 Chorus

Wild and free, or home with me,
Every animal you can see!
Big or small, they all belong,
Animals make our world so strong! 🌍

🐄 Domestic Animal — Cow

Moo moo, cow upon the farm,
Giving milk with gentle charm,
Black and white upon the hill,
Chewing grass so calm and still!

🐕 Domestic Animal — Dog

Woof woof, doggy at the door,
Wagging tail and wanting more,
Best of friends through every day,
Come on puppy, let's go play!

🐱 Domestic Animal — Cat

Meow meow, kitty on the chair,
Softly purring without a care,
Curling up beside the fire,
Cat's the pet we all admire!

🐓 Domestic Animal — Hen

Cluck cluck, little hen so bright,
Laying eggs from morning light,
Pecking grain across the yard,
Busy hen works oh so hard!

🐑 Domestic Animal — Sheep

Baa baa sheep upon the hill,
Wooly coat so soft to feel,
Follow me where green grass grows,
Watch the shepherd as he goes!

🌟 Final Chorus

Wild and free, or home with me,
Every animal you can see!
Love them all both big and small,
Animals are friends to all! 🐾

👨‍👩‍👧 Parents' Corner

Why this animals poem is great for your child's learning

🧠 Skills Developed

  • Animal vocabulary — names & sounds
  • Wild vs domestic categorisation
  • Phonics through rhyme patterns
  • Memory and recall skills
  • Nature awareness & curiosity

📚 Learning Outcomes

  • Recognise 14+ animals by name
  • Match animals to their sounds
  • Understand habitat differences
  • Improve listening comprehension
  • Build confidence through repetition

💡 Activity Tips

  • Act out animal sounds together!
  • Visit a farm or zoo after reading
  • Sort animal toys: wild vs domestic
  • Draw your favourite animal
  • Ask: "Which is your favourite?" 🐾
