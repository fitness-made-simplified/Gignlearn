📖 The Stories

Story 1🥭

Gigloo Shares His Mango

Gigloo has one big juicy mango — but his friend is hungry. What will he do? A warm story about the joy of sharing.

💛 Moral: Sharing is caring
Story 2🆘

Gigloo Asks for Help

Gigloo gets stuck and feels too proud to ask for help. He learns that asking for help is brave, not weak!

💙 Moral: It's okay to ask for help
Story 3✅

Gigloo Tells the Truth

Gigloo accidentally breaks a flower pot. He's scared to admit it — but discovers that honesty always feels better.

💚 Moral: Always tell the truth
Story 4🌑

Gigloo Faces the Dark

Gigloo is afraid of the dark. One night, he must be brave. A gentle story helping kids understand courage.

💜 Moral: Be brave — face your fears
Story 5🙏

Gigloo Says Sorry

Gigloo says something unkind to his friend. He feels bad inside. He learns that a real sorry can fix everything.

🩷 Moral: Saying sorry heals hearts

Why these stories are perfect for your child

🧠 Skills Developed

  • Emotional intelligence (EQ)

🌟 Values Taught

  • Honesty & integrity
  • Bravery & facing fears
  • Accountability & saying sorry

  • Print & read together daily
