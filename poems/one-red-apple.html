Poem Lyrics 🍎

Read every line with your child — press the button and Gigloo will read it for you! Each number lights up as we go!

1
One red apple on the tree,
Round and shiny, just for me!
2
Two blue birds sing with glee,
Flapping wings so wild and free!
3
Three green frogs jump so high,
Leaping up towards the sky!
4
Four bright stars light the night,
Twinkling, twinkling, shining bright!
5
Five purple flowers in a row,
Swaying gently, watch them grow!
6
Six orange fish swim the sea,
Splashing waves so happily!
7
Seven white clouds float up high,
Drifting softly through the sky!
8
Eight pink butterflies flutter by,
Dancing colours catch my eye!
9
Nine grey elephants march along,
Stomping feet and singing a song!
10
Ten little children clap and say,
"We love counting every day!" 🎉

What Your Child Learns

Every GigNLearn poem is built around a clear learning goal. Here's what this poem teaches!

🔢

Numbers Learned

  • Counting 1 through 10
  • Number words (One, Two, Three...)
  • Number–quantity connection
  • Sequential order of numbers
⭐

Shapes & Colours

  • Colours: red, blue, green, yellow, purple
  • Round (circle) — apple shape
  • Recognition of natural shapes
  • Visual discrimination skills
🧠

Skills Developed

  • Early numeracy & counting
  • Rhyme & phonemic awareness
  • Vocabulary — animals & nature
  • Memory through rhythm & repetition
⏱️

Usage Guide

  • Read time: ~4 minutes
  • Activity time: ~20 minutes
  • Best for: Ages 2–7 years
  • Repeat daily for fluency

💡 Try This Activity at Home!

After reading, go on a "Number Hunt" around your house! Ask your child: "Can you find ONE red thing? TWO blue things? THREE green things?" This connects the poem's counting directly to real objects — making numbers stick much faster! 🏠🔢
