Poem Lyrics 🌈

Read every colourful line with your child — press the button and let Gigloo march through the rainbow!

🍎
Red the apple marched in line,
Crunchy, juicy, oh so fine!
🥕
Orange the carrot gave a cheer,
With a bunny hop and floppy ear!
🍌
Yellow banana did a twirl,
Swishing, swaying in a swirl!
🥦
Green the broccoli stood up tall,
Tiny tree — the best of all!
🫐
Blue blueberries bounced with glee,
Round and sweet as they can be!
🍇
Indigo grapes danced in a bunch,
Juicy, plump — the perfect lunch!
🌸
Violet plum bowed with a smile,
Leading the parade in style!
🌈
All the colours, bright and true,
Make a rainbow just for you!
Eat the rainbow every day,
Keep the snack parade on its way! 🎉

What Your Child Learns

A rainbow of learning in every stanza!

🌈

Colours Learned

  • Red, Orange, Yellow, Green
  • Blue, Indigo, Violet (ROYGBIV)
  • Colour names + spelling
  • Colour recognition in real foods
🥕

Healthy Eating

  • Fruits: Apple, Banana, Blueberry, Grapes, Plum
  • Vegetables: Carrot, Broccoli
  • "Eat the rainbow" concept

🧠

Skills Developed

  • Colour identification & sorting
  • Rhyme & phonics awareness
  • Vocabulary — foods & colours
  • Memory through rhythm
⏱️

Usage Guide

  • Read time: ~4 minutes
  • Activity time: ~20 minutes
  • Best for: Ages 2–7 years
  • Link to snack time for best results!

💡 Rainbow Snack Activity at Home!

During snack time, recreate the parade! Put out 3–4 coloured fruits or veggies and ask your child to name the colour before eating each one. Say the poem line together — "Red the apple marched in line!" — then bite the apple! This connects the poem to real food in a fun, memorable way. 🌈🍽️
