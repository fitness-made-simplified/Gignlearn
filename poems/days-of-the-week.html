What Your Child Learns

7 days + a spooky theme = maximum engagement!

📅

Days Learned

  • All 7 days in correct order
  • Spelling of each day name
  • Weekend vs Weekday concept
  • Monday = start, Sunday = rest
🎃

Why Halloween Theme?

  • Fun theme = better memory retention
  • Kids are more engaged with exciting visuals
  • Seasonal celebration awareness
  • Creative learning environment
🧠

Skills Developed

  • Sequential memory — day order
  • Rhythmic learning through song
  • Time & calendar awareness
  • Vocabulary — day names
⏱️

Usage Guide

  • Sing every morning at breakfast
  • Ask "What day is today?" daily
  • Best for: Ages 3–8 years
  • Great for LKG, UKG, Grade 1

💡 Daily Calendar Activity!

Every morning, ask your child to sing the days song and stop at today's day. Then ask: "What was yesterday? What will tomorrow be?" Print a weekly calendar from the PDF and let them colour each day as it passes. By the end of the week they'll know the order perfectly — and look forward to doing it again next Monday! 📅🎃
