Why This Workbook?

Everything a nursery child needs — in one beautiful book!

📚

Topics Covered

  • Greetings — Hello, Please, Thank You, Sorry
  • Alphabet — A to Z recognition
  • Shapes — Square, Rectangle, Triangle, Circle
  • Colours — Blue, Red, Green, Yellow & more
  • Fun activities & colouring pages
🧠

Skills Developed

  • Social-emotional learning (greetings)
  • Letter recognition — phonics readiness
  • Shape recognition — early geometry
  • Colour identification & naming
  • Fine motor skills — colouring & writing
🎯

Perfect For

  • Nursery & Preschool children
  • Ages 3–6 years
  • Home learning & school homework
  • Summer learning activity
  • Classroom resource for teachers
🖨️

How to Use

  • Download the PDF — free, one click
  • Print on A4 paper (colour or B&W)
  • Sit with your child — do one page a day
  • Laminate for reuse!
  • Great as a daily activity book

💡 Make It a Daily Learning Time!

Set aside 15–20 minutes each morning as "Workbook Time" with your child. Do one section per day — Greetings on Monday, Alphabet on Tuesday, Shapes on Wednesday, Colours on Thursday, and colouring on Friday! By the end of the week your child will have covered everything in the book. Print a new copy next week and watch how much faster they go! 📅🐯
