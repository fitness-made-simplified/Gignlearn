LAND TRANSPORT

Cars go fast, beep beep beep,
Buses big, with seats so deep.
Trains go choo-choo down the track,
Taxis zoom, then hurry back.
Bikes go vroom vroom on the street,
Scooters roll with happy feet.
Trucks go rumble, loud and strong,
They can carry things along.
Jeep goes bounce on bumpy ground,
Vans go vroom with rolling sound.
All these rides move here and there,
🚗 Land transport takes us everywhere!
✈️

AIR TRANSPORT

Zoom zoom, planes go high,
Up up in the blue sky!
Wings flap, engines roar,
Flying fast, more and more!
Helicopter spin spin round,
Chop chop, up from ground!
Jet jet, streak so quick,
Air fun, pick pick pick!
Yay yay, fly with me,
✈️ Air transport, whee whee whee!
🚢

WATER TRANSPORT

Splash splash, boats go float,
On the water, toot toot boat!
Sail sail, wind blows strong,
Row row, sing a song!
Yay yay, swim with me,
🚢 Water transport, splashy glee!

What Your Child Learns

Three poems, three types of transport, endless learning!

🚗

Transport Vocabulary

  • Land: Car, Bus, Train, Taxi, Bike, Truck, Jeep, Van, Scooter
  • Air: Plane, Helicopter, Jet
  • Water: Boat, Sail, Ship, Rowboat
🧠

Concepts Learned

  • Classification — Land / Air / Water
  • Onomatopoeia — vroom, zoom, splash, choo-choo
  • Rhyming word patterns
  • Scientific categories of transport
🎯

Curriculum Fit

  • EVS — Means of Transport
  • English — Vocabulary building
  • Science — How things move
  • Preschool, LKG, UKG, Grade 1
⏱️

Usage Guide

  • Read time: ~6 minutes (all 3)
  • Activity time: ~25 minutes
  • Best for: Ages 3–8 years
  • Ideal for EVS class activity

💡 Transport Sorting Game at Home!

Draw 3 circles on paper — label them 🚗 Land, ✈️ Air, 🚢 Water. Cut out pictures of vehicles from old magazines or print them. Ask your child to sort each vehicle into the right circle. Then sing the poem together for each group! This hands-on activity cements the concept brilliantly. 🎯
