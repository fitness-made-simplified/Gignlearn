💡 Vowel Hunt Activity!

After completing the worksheet, go on a "Vowel Hunt" around the house! Ask your child to find one object starting with each vowel — an Apple in the kitchen, an Egg in the fridge, something Orange, something that goes Under something, and so on! This cements the vowel-word connection through real-world experience. 🔍🏠
